Potato Cassarole

Potato Cassarole Recipe with Roquez

Description

Hot and Filling Potato Cassarole that your family will love! This recipe should be added to your cookbooks!

Ingredients

  • 8 Potatoes (Large) - Cooked
  • 1 Onion Chopped
  • 16oz Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 or 1/2 Stick of Butter
  • 8oz Sour Cream
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
  • Stick of Celery Chopped (1/4 cup)
  • 1 Can cream of celery soup

Steps/ Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Stir ingredients into large bowl
  3. Add ingredietns in large cooking pan
  4. Put in oven and bake for 25 to 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
