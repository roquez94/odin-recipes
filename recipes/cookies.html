Cookies

Description

Here's a Walker house favorite! Warm and delicious chocolate chip cookies! Easy to follow recipe will make about a dozen cookies. Hope you and your family will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Chocolate Chips
  • 1/2 Cup of White Sugar
  • 3/4 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Unsalted Butter Melted
  • 1 Whole Egg + 1 Egg yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 and a half cups all purpose Flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Steps / Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking soda
  2. In another bowl, mix sugars and melted butter
  3. Add eggs w/yolk and vanilla extract
  4. Add flour to wet mix
  5. Fold in Chocolate
  6. Cover and chill in fridge for 30 mins
